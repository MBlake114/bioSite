What is crochet? Well the general idea is to use hooks (of varying sizes), a ball of wool/yarn, some scissors, and a plan to create almost anything you can think of. Echo has created a ton of really unique items including the above images. Crochet is a really great way to express creative and can even be a cathartic stress relief.
